About Kue Lestari

Welcome to Kue Lestari, a home-based cake business dedicated to bringing you the most delicious and beautifully crafted cakes.

Our passion for baking started from a simple love of creating sweet treats for family and friends. What began as a hobby has grown into a beloved local business, specializing in custom cakes for all occasions.

We use only the finest ingredients, ensuring every cake is fresh, flavorful, and made with care. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, or just craving something sweet, we're here to make your day special.
